Publication

Graphite, finds extensive industrial applications, such as in refractories and batteries. The increasing demand for high-purity, large-flake graphite is driven by electric vehicle adoption, projected to surge by over 700% by 2025. Given the scarcity of high-grade natural graphite ores, processing low-grade ores is essential to meet this demand. Flotation, involves various techniques including pretreatment processes, liberation/grinding methods, and different flotation reagents and methods. The review also delves into a range of flotation reagents, including eco-friendly alternatives, used for graphite separation. Read complete review paper published in International Journal of Chemical Engineering on 21st March 2023.
